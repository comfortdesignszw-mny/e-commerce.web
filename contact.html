Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using PayNow, EcoCash, InnBucks, Bank Transfer, or Cash on Delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayNow, EcoCash, InnBucks, Bank Transfer, and Cash on Delivery. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

How long does shipping take?

For physical products, we offer free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for physical products. Items must be in original condition. Digital products are non-refundable unless there's a technical issue.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order in your account dashboard.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, our customer support team is available Monday-Friday 8AM-6PM. You can reach us via phone, email, or live chat on our website.
